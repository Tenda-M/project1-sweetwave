About Me

Hey there, my name is Tenda. I like to try out new recipes in my spare time, usually in the evenings when my kids are asleep. My goal at SweetWave is to bring joy to everyday baking, as we see it as essential for the well-being of individuals, communities, and the environment. I support homecooks globally in connecting and assisting one another through recipe sharing and cooking advice.


My Kitchen

The kitchen is both a science lab and an artist's studio, where I work hard to produce easy-to-make, delicious recipes for you, thank you for checking this site out.


Influence

My method is mainly influenced by a blend of delicious and nutritious outlooks from my personal life encounters, resulting in a diverse range. I trust you discover the website beneficial and return!
